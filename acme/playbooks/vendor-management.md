# Vendor Management Playbook

## Vendor Intake
- Gather requirements and scope.
- Collect security and compliance details.
- Identify key stakeholders and approvers.

## Evaluation
- Compare pricing and service levels.
- Review contract terms and renewal dates.
- Document risks and mitigation plans.

## Ongoing Management
- Schedule quarterly business reviews.
- Track performance metrics and SLAs.
- Maintain a log of issues and resolutions.
- Set up alerts for contract renewal deadlines
